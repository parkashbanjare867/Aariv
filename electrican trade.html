Electrician Trade Introduction

Electrician trade mein students ko electrical wiring, installation, maintenance aur repair ka training diya jata hai.

Course Duration & Structure

  • Total Duration – 2 Years
  • 4 Semesters
  • Theory + Practical Training

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10th Pass (Mathematics & Science)
  • Minimum Age – 14 Years

Detailed Electrician Syllabus

  • Basic Electricity
  • Electrical Wiring
  • Transformer
  • Motor Winding
  • Electrical Measuring Instruments
  • Domestic Appliances

Skills Developed in Electrician

  • Wiring Installation
  • Fault Finding
  • Maintenance Work
  • Safety Handling

Tools & Machines Used

  • Tester
  • Multimeter
  • Pliers
  • Drill Machine
  • Wire Stripper
