FITTER Trade Introduction

Fitter ITI ka ek mechanical trade hai jisme students ko machine parts banana, assemble karna, repair aur maintenance ka kaam sikhaya jata hai. Yeh trade industry aur manufacturing field me kaafi important hota hai.

Course Duration & Structure

  • Total Duration – 2 Year
  • 4 Semester System
  • Theory + Practical Training
  • Workshop Practice

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Qualification – 10th Pass
  • Maths & Science Knowledge Helpful
  • Physical Fitness Required

Detailed FITTER Syllabus

  • Engineering Drawing
  • Workshop Calculation
  • Measuring Tools Use
  • Hand Tools Training
  • Machine Assembly
  • Drilling, Grinding & Cutting
  • Pipe Fitting
  • Maintenance Work

Skills Developed in FITTER

  • Machine Handling Skills
  • Technical Drawing Reading
  • Maintenance & Repair Skills
  • Measurement Accuracy
  • Safety Awareness

Tools & Machines Used

  • Vernier Caliper
  • Micrometer
  • Drilling Machine
  • Grinding Machine
  • Hand Tools (Hammer, Spanner, File)

Career Opportunities After FITTER

  • Machine Fitter
  • Maintenance Technician
  • Production Technician
  • Railway Jobs
  • Factory & Industry Jobs
  • Self Workshop Business

Salary Scope

Fitter trade karne ke baad starting salary approx ₹12,000 se ₹25,000 per month ho sakti hai. Experience badhne par salary aur promotion mil sakta hai.

Advantages of FITTER Trade

  • Industrial Job Opportunities
  • Government Sector Chances
  • Technical Skill Development
  • Self Employment Opportunity

Higher Study Options

  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
  • Apprenticeship Training
  • Advanced Technical Courses
  • Industrial Safety Course
